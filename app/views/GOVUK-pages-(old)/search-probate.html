Search for probate records

You can search for a probate record in England and Wales online or by post.

Search online for a probate record

You can search online for records for people who died after 1857. You can also order copies for £10 each.

You can also get notified when a grant of probate is issued if the person died within the last 6 months.

It can take up to 10 working days to receive your documents.

A new probate record will be online approximately 14 days after probate has been issued.

Search for a probate record by post

Download and fill in form PA1S - Application for a search (copies of grants and wills). Send it to the address on the form.

It costs £10. This includes a copy of the probate and the will (if there is one).

You should get a response within 4 weeks.
