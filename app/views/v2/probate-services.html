Probate services

Probate gives you the legal right to deal with a person’s property, money and possessions (their ‘estate’) after they’ve died.

Apply for probate

You can complete and pay for an application for probate online. You may also need to post some supporting documents. We'll let you know what you need to send when you've completed your application.

Make an application for probate


Stop an application for probate

Use this service to stop an application for a grant of probate. This is also known as 'entering a caveat'.

Stop an application for probate


Get notified when a grant of probate is issued

You can get a copy of someone's will and grant of probate when it's been issued by the probate registry. All grants of probate are public records.
