Apply for probate

Probate gives you the legal right to deal with a person’s property, money and possessions (their ‘estate’) after they’ve died.

You can complete and pay for an application for probate online. You may also need to post some supporting documents. We'll let you know what you need to send when you've completed your application.

Joint applications

If more than one person is dealing with the estate you can only apply online if the person who died left a will.

You must tell the other executors that you’re naming them in your application. They’ll be asked to confirm online that the information you provide is correct.

The other applying executors need to have:

  • seen the original will
  • a mobile phone and email address
  • access to the internet

Fees

It costs £215 to apply for probate if the value of the estate is more than £5,000. It’s free if the value is less.

Extra copies of the grant of probate cost 50p each.

You’ll need to pay online by debit or credit card.
