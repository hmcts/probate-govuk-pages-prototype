Get notified when a grant of probate is issued

You can get a copy of someone's will and grant of probate when it's been issued by the probate registry. All grants of probate are public records.

Your application will last for 6 months. If the grant of probate has not been issued in that time, you can apply for another 6 months.

You will need:

  • the full name of the person who died (and any other names they were known by)
  • the exact date they died

Fees

It costs £10 to apply. Extra copies of the will and grant cost 50p.

You'll need to pay online by debit or credit card.
